the story so far

by treeherder

Posted on November 19, 2013 at 11:00 PM

this is Brian, David, and Greg. Greg basically put this group together based on our common interest in permaculture and the potential for reclaiming unused urban space for cultivating food. We met David through our first experiment with aquaponics (many, many, many home depot trips) and we started with meeting sort of informally but regularly for lunch on the weekends to talk about various questions of sustainability and the role DIY- or Open-Source technology can play in making these strategies available to people who have in interest in trying to live that way. We often talk about tiny houses and solar cells, and Greg is always trying to cook stuff with mirrors and lenses. I don't actually know much about Brian yet, but he's a good worker, and I look forward to him coming again next week.
