recclaim fallow urban land

our goal is to provide the means for communities to rehabilitate unused space in urban environments. We will provide comprehensive documentation about the process of recclamation and rehabilitation, and the long-term upkeep of commuunity garden plots

plant-based nutrition education

we hope to use this space to assemble a database of information on plant-based nutrition, as well as means by which to expose communities to this information. We will graph relevant datapoints with some innovative and engaging displays that can be manipulated to calculate the overall need for a group of people, based on the gaps in their staple diet

seedling distribution

this page runs on a microcomputer that is also the grow-controller for our seedling incubator. This incubator is an automatically optimized chamber that controls every aspect of plant development. we are building the system on an open-source platform, and hope to provide comprehensive documentation for reproducing the apparatus.
